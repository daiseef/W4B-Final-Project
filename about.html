Let us introduce ourselves!


Wixton Candle Co. was founded in October 2021 by Daisee Fuentes. She, along with her partner, Chris, wanted to provide luxurious, hand-made candles free of toxins. After months of research and testing a variety of ingredients, they settled on a coconut soy wax that provided a strong scent throw and ensured a clean burn. They decided to pair this wax with a wooden wick that produced a crackling sound as it burned. Finally, they sourced fragrance oils that were non-toxic, phthalate-free and delivered strong scents. All of these ingredients come together to provide our customers with the safest and best possible product. As Wixton continues to grow, we look forward to coming up with new products for everyone to enjoy.
